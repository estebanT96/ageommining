Mining Engineering

Detailed evaluation of mining projects, short, medium, and long-term design and planning, along with topographic surveys and leach pad design.

Our team of experts, established since 2012, provides comprehensive solutions for both surface and underground mining planning and operation. We focus on achieving the optimal balance between operational safety and economic profitability, applying high levels of technical skill in the evaluation of national and international mining projects. Our scope ranges from initial pre-feasibility studies to advanced management of critical infrastructure and technical personnel training.

  • Feasibility Studies: Execution of pre-feasibility and technical-economic feasibility analysis.
  • Operational Planning: Detailed design and engineering for short, medium, and long-term operations.
  • Infrastructure Design: Technical planning of waste areas (dumps) and leach pads.
  • Geometric Control: High-precision topographic surveys for project monitoring.

Geomechanics

Focus on 2D and 3D stability analysis, pre-feasibility and feasibility geotechnical modeling, as well as specialized instrumentation and monitoring in surface and underground mines.

We implement an integrated geomechanical methodology that combines structural and geotechnical field surveys with specialized rock mechanics laboratory testing. This integration allows us to determine and calibrate representative design parameters of the rock mass (UCS, deformation modulus, GSI, RMR, Q, among others), reducing geotechnical uncertainty. Based on the correlation between observed behavior in excavations and laboratory results, we adjust support criteria and fortification schemes according to real ground conditions, optimizing stability, deformation control, and operational efficiency.

  • Stability Modeling: 2D and 3D rock mass analysis through advanced numerical modeling.
  • Specialized Instrumentation: Installation and supervision of extensometers, piezometers, and smart support.
  • Structural Analysis: Stability evaluation for tailings dams, leach tanks, and waste deposits.
  • Field Characterization: Supervision and logging of oriented boreholes and routine geotechnical mapping.
  • Laboratory Services: Specialized rock mechanics testing for the determination of design parameters.

Geology

Generation of 3D geological models, resource estimation through block modeling, and execution of drilling campaigns with high-precision lithological and structural logging.

We provide high-precision geological services focused on generating 3D models that facilitate strategic decision-making. Our methodology includes everything from lithological and structural mapping in the field to advanced resource estimation using block modeling. We specialize in transparent and validated database management, allowing for rigorous ore control and accurate interpretation of exploration targets.

  • 3D Modeling and Reserves: Creation of 3D geological models and resource estimation through block modeling.
  • Exploration and Mapping: Drilling campaigns, geochemical prospecting, and detailed lithological-structural mapping.
  • Data Management: Management, validation, and technical auditing of geological databases.
  • Ore Control: Implementation of ore control protocols to optimize extraction.

Hydrogeology

Regional and local studies including hydrological balances, pumping tests, numerical modeling, and design of water management strategies in mining environments.

We develop regional and local hydrogeological studies designed to manage water resources efficiently and sustainably in the mining environment. Our technical capacity allows us to perform everything from complex hydrological balances to numerical aquifer modeling to predict operational impact. We offer comprehensive solutions in well design, construction, and maintenance, always ensuring compliance with the physical-chemical and water parameters required by regulations.

  • Hydrological Modeling: Development of conceptual and numerical hydrogeological models for flow simulation.
  • Well Management: Location, technical design, construction supervision, and monitoring of extraction wells.
  • Field Tests: Execution of pumping tests, Lugeon/Lefranc tests, and water quality sampling.
  • Deposit Characterization: Specific hydrological balances for leach pads, tailings, and dumps.
  • Mitigation and Waterproofing: Design of grouting campaigns and groundwater control strategies.

Geophysics

Application of geophysics for groundwater exploration, soil analysis, terrestrial and aerial magnetometry, and advanced downhole camera inspection.

We apply cutting-edge geophysical methods for non-invasive resource prospecting and technical subsurface characterization. Our experience covers groundwater exploration, aerial and terrestrial magnetometry, and the application of geophysics oriented toward geotechnics. Through the rigorous interpretation of seismic and ground-penetrating radar (GPR) data, we provide critical information about ground structure, optimizing safety in the construction of mining infrastructure.

  • Water Exploration: Specialized application of geophysics for the location of aquifers and groundwater.
  • Seismic Methods and GPR: Use of refraction seismic and ground-penetrating radar services for soil and geotechnical analysis.
  • Magnetometry: Execution and interpretation of terrestrial and aerial magnetometry campaigns.
  • Technical Inspection: Visual well evaluation using high-resolution cameras to verify structural integrity.
